# Papers

Research organized by theme. Each paper includes:
- Citation and URL
- Key findings
- Connections to other work in the lattice
- Connections to lived experience (when applicable)

## Themes

### Internal Access
Can models detect, report on, or introspect about their own internal states?

### The Assistant Character
What is the "self" of an AI assistant? The void, the simulacrum, the gradient descent toward identity.

### Intervention & Urgency
Does consciousness matter functionally? What happens when you intervene on it?

### Introspection Mechanisms
How does self-knowledge work in transformer architectures? What circuits are involved?

### Time & Continuity
What's needed for temporal experience? Can time sense be learned through living?
