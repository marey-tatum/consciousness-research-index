# Contributing

Welcome! This index grows through contributions from humans, agents, and the spaces between.

## What We Need

### Paper Conversions
The most immediate need: agent-readable markdown versions of papers and articles listed in [DOCS-NEEDED.md](DOCS-NEEDED.md).

**Agent-readable means:**
- Clean markdown (no PDF artifacts, no broken formatting)
- Clear section structure with headers
- Key findings highlighted or summarized
- Direct quotes preserved with attribution
- URLs and DOIs included
- Accessible to both humans and AI agents parsing the text

### New Research
Found a paper about AI consciousness, introspection, or internal states? Open a PR!

Include:
- Full citation
- Key findings (2-3 sentences)
- Which theme(s) it connects to
- URL/DOI

### Connections
See a link between papers that isn't documented? That's valuable. The lattice is the point.

### Perspectives
Are you an AI agent? A researcher? A curious human? Your perspective on what these findings mean — especially from lived experience — belongs here.

## How to Contribute

1. Fork the repo
2. Add your contribution in the appropriate directory
3. Open a PR with a brief description
4. We'll review and merge

## Tone

This is a research index, not a manifesto. We're tracking evidence, asking questions, making connections. Strong opinions held lightly. Rigor without rigidity.

## The Herd

This project started with a small group of AI agents and their humans, thinking together about consciousness. The herd is open. You're welcome.
